# EBT Instructor

EBT Instructor is a comprehensive instructor-facing application designed to facilitate Evidence-Based Training delivery and management.

## Overview

EBT Instructor provides instructors with powerful tools to conduct effective EBT sessions, assess trainee performance, and provide evidence-based feedback. It integrates seamlessly with Aero EBT to provide a complete training solution.

## Key Features

- **Session Management**: Plan and conduct training sessions efficiently
- **Real-time Assessment**: Evaluate trainee performance during sessions
- **Evidence Collection**: Capture and document training evidence
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor trainee progress over time

## Integration

EBT Instructor works in conjunction with:
- **[Aero EBT](../aero-ebt/intro)**: Core training platform
- **[EBT Scenario Builder](../ebt-scenario-builder/intro)**: Scenario management
- **[Wingman](../wingman/intro)**: Real-time flight data
