# Safety Department

Implementation roadmap specifically for the Safety Department focusing on safety protocols and reporting.

## Overview

The Safety Department ensures EBT Instructor supports safety management processes, incident reporting, and safety compliance throughout the organization.

## Focus Areas

### Safety Protocols
- Safety management system integration
- Safety procedure configuration
- Risk assessment workflows
- Incident reporting setup
- Safety compliance monitoring

### Reporting
- Safety incident reporting
- Safety data analysis
- Compliance reporting
- Trend analysis
- Regulatory safety reporting

## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1: Safety Requirements (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Review safety requirements
- [ ] Assess current safety processes
- [ ] Identify safety integration points
- [ ] Document safety protocols
- [ ] Plan safety configuration

### Phase 2: Safety Configuration (Week 2-4)
- [ ] Configure safety workflows
- [ ] Set up incident reporting
- [ ] Configure risk assessment processes
- [ ] Set up safety compliance monitoring
- [ ] Configure safety notifications

### Phase 3: Integration Setup (Week 4-5)
- [ ] Integrate with safety management systems
- [ ] Connect incident reporting systems
- [ ] Set up safety data synchronization
- [ ] Configure safety dashboards
- [ ] Test safety workflows

### Phase 4: Testing and Validation (Week 5-6)
- [ ] Test incident reporting
- [ ] Validate risk assessment workflows
- [ ] Review safety compliance reports
- [ ] Test safety notifications
- [ ] Conduct safety validation

### Phase 5: Training and Deployment (Week 6-7)
- [ ] Train safety staff
- [ ] Conduct safety pilot program
- [ ] Gather safety feedback
- [ ] Refine safety processes
- [ ] Prepare for full deployment

### Phase 6: Safety Operations (Week 7+)
- [ ] Monitor safety processes
- [ ] Review safety incident reports
- [ ] Conduct safety audits
- [ ] Maintain safety documentation
- [ ] Continuous safety improvement

## Key Milestones

- **Week 2**: Safety requirements documented
- **Week 4**: Safety configuration complete
- **Week 5**: Integration operational
- **Week 6**: Safety testing validated
- **Week 7**: Safety staff trained
- **Week 8**: Safety operations active
