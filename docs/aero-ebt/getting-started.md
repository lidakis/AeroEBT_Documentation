# Getting Started with Aero EBT

This guide will walk you through the initial setup and configuration of Aero EBT.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- Access credentials provided by your administrator
- A supported web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An active internet connection
- Required permissions for your training organization

## Initial Setup

### Step 1: Access the Platform

1. Navigate to the Aero EBT login page
2. Enter your credentials provided by your administrator
3. Complete the initial setup wizard if prompted

### Step 2: Configure Your Profile

1. Navigate to **Settings** â†’ **Profile**
2. Complete your user profile information
3. Set your preferences for notifications and reports

### Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

The main dashboard provides:
- Overview of training activities
- Recent performance metrics
- Quick access to common tasks
- System announcements

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during setup:

1. Verify your credentials are correct
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact your system administrator
