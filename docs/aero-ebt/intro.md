# Aero EBT

Aero EBT is SkyDynamics' comprehensive Evidence-Based Training (EBT) platform designed to revolutionize aviation training through data-driven insights and advanced analytics.

## Overview

Aero EBT provides a centralized platform for managing, analyzing, and improving pilot training programs based on evidence and performance data. It enables training organizations to move beyond traditional training methods to more effective, personalized training approaches.

## Key Capabilities

- **Evidence-Based Analysis**: Make training decisions based on real performance data
- **Comprehensive Reporting**: Generate detailed reports on training effectiveness
- **Integration Ready**: Seamlessly integrates with other SkyDynamics products
- **Scalable Architecture**: Supports training organizations of all sizes
