# Wingman

Wingman is an advanced flight safety and monitoring companion designed to provide real-time decision support and enhanced situational awareness.

## Overview

Wingman serves as your intelligent copilot, offering real-time analysis, alerts, and recommendations to enhance flight safety and operational efficiency. It integrates seamlessly with the Aero EBT ecosystem to provide comprehensive flight monitoring and training data.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Continuous flight parameter monitoring
- **Decision Support**: AI-powered recommendations
- **Safety Alerts**: Proactive hazard identification
- **Performance Analytics**: In-flight performance tracking

## Benefits

- Enhanced situational awareness
- Improved decision-making
- Reduced pilot workload
- Better safety outcomes

## Integration

Wingman integrates with:
- **[Aero EBT](../aero-ebt/intro)**: Training data and analytics
- **[EBT Instructor](../ebt-instructor/intro)**: Training session support
- Flight systems and avionics
