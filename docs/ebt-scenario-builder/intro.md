# EBT Scenario Builder

EBT Scenario Builder is a powerful tool for creating, customizing, and managing Evidence-Based Training scenarios.

## Overview

EBT Scenario Builder enables training organizations to develop tailored training scenarios that align with their specific training objectives and regulatory requirements. Create realistic, challenging scenarios that enhance pilot training effectiveness.

## Key Features

- **Scenario Creation**: Build custom training scenarios from scratch
- **Template Library**: Access pre-built scenario templates
- **Customization**: Modify scenarios to meet specific needs
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate with Aero EBT and EBT Instructor

## Use Cases

- Create scenarios for specific training objectives
- Adapt standard scenarios to local conditions
- Build scenario libraries for training programs
- Share scenarios across training organizations

## Integration

EBT Scenario Builder integrates with:
- **[Aero EBT](../aero-ebt/intro)**: Scenario deployment and management
- **[EBT Instructor](../ebt-instructor/intro)**: Scenario execution
